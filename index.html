Alpine Adventures: The Legend of the Ski Fox

The Tale

High in the mystical peaks of the Eternal Snow Mountains, there lives a remarkable creature known as the Ski Fox. Legend has it that this extraordinary fox learned to ski by observing the ancient mountain spirits who would glide down the slopes collecting magical snowflakes to maintain the balance of winter.

One day, the mountain spirits vanished, leaving the task of collecting snowflakes to their faithful observer. Now, as their chosen successor, the Ski Fox must gather these enchanted crystals while avoiding the treacherous depths below. The spirits occasionally send their silver airplanes to help create new paths, ensuring the Ski Fox can reach even the highest snowflakes.

How to Play

  • Movement: Use the left and right arrow keys to move
  • Jump: Press the up arrow key to jump
  • Platforms: Land on the green platforms to avoid falling
  • Double Jump: Collect red snowflakes to gain double jump power

Scoring System

  • Regular Snowflakes: 1-4 points (size indicates value)
  • Golden Snowflakes: 10 points (largest in size)
  • Red Snowflakes: 1 point + Double Jump ability
  • Airplanes: Touch them to create new platforms

Pro Tips

  • Watch for golden snowflakes - they're worth the most points!
  • Use double jumps strategically to reach higher areas
  • Create new paths by intercepting the silver airplanes
  • Larger snowflakes are worth more points - prioritize them when possible
